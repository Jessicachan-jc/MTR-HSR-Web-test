Ferris Wheel (Eye of Tianjin)

Address：
Yongle Brideg, Sanchahekou, Hongqiao District, Tianjin

Content：
"The Yongle Bridge Ferris Wheel" is the full name for "Eye of Tianjin". It is the only wheel in the world which is built on a bridge across the river, serving both sightseeing and traffic functions. The Ferris wheel is 110 metres in diameter, which is equivalent to the height of a 35-storey building. It can carry up to 384 visitors at a time.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Tianjinxi Station, take Bus 910/904 towards Huaihe Road Bus Station. Get off at Yongleqiao Bus Station and walk for about 7 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 11-minute taxi ride from Tianjinxi Station.

The Porcelain House

Address：
72 Chifeng Road, Heping District, Tianjin

Content：
Located at 72 Chifeng Road, The Porcelain House was formerly a centuries-old French-style building. The exterior of the house is decorated with over 700 million pieces of precious ancient porcelain, 13,000 ancient chinaware and porcelain plates as well as over 300 decorative stone statues from various eras. The number of ancient tiles used and the giant porcelain dragon in the building have both broken the Guinness World Records.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Tianjinxi Station, walk for about 3 minutes to Xizhan Metro Station. Take Metro Line 1 towards Lilou and get off at Yingkoudao Station. From Exit A, walk for about 15 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 23-minute taxi ride from Tianjinxi Station.

Tianjin Five Great Avenues

Address：
64 Chongqing Road, Heping District, Tianjin

Content：
With Tianjin people calling it the "World Architecture Museum", The Fifth Avenue is actually the former concession and the most well-preserved foreign style building complex in China. Along the five streets, there are over 2,000 garden villas with different architectural styles dating from the 1920s and 1930s. Visitors can take a carriage ride to explore this western-style neighbourhood.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Tianjinxi Station, walk for about 3 minutes to Xizhan Metro Station. Take Metro Line 1 towards Lilou. Get off at Xiaobailou Station and walk for about 7 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 35-minute taxi ride from Tianjinxi Station.

Italian Style Town

Address：
Intersection of Shengli Road and Ziyou Road, Hebei District, Tianjin

Content：
The Italian-style neighbourhood used to be the Italian concession. The area is said to be the only well-preserved Italian architectural complex in Asia, with nearly 200 buildings. In addition to the famous residences of eminent people such as Liang Qichao and Feng Guozhang, there are also a lot of notable attractions such as the Marco Polo Road, Dante Square, Century Clock and Sacred Heart Church.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Tianjinxi Station, walk for about 3 minutes to Xizhan Metro Station. Take Metro Line 6 towards Meilinlu. Get off at Changhonggongyuan Station and change to Line 2 towards Binhai International Airport. Get off at Dongnanjiao Station and walk for about 13 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 16-minute taxi ride from Tianjinxi Station.

Ancient Culture Street

Address：
Nankaitongbei Road, Tianjin

Content：
Ancient Culture Street is planned to mimic the shops in Qing Dynasty of China. In 1986, this area was developed into a pedestrian street with nearly 100 shops. Nowadays, it becomes a paradise for foodies as people can find different Tianjin's local delicacies and snacks there such as the Goubuli Buns and Tianjin Mahua.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Tianjinxi Station, take Bus 15 towards Wujiang Beili. Get off at Dongma Road Station and walk for about 7 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 16-minute taxi ride from Tianjinxi Station.
