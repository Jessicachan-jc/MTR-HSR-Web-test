Hongya Cave

Address：
88 Jialing Jiang Binjiang Road, Yuzhong District, Chongqing

Content：
Decorated with faux wood, the appearance of Hongya Cave inherits the stilt houses of Linjiang in the 1940s. The beautiful butterfly tiles and stone pavements re-enact the traditional architectural style. After remediation in 2006, it was reopened and transformed into a food and leisure street area with Chongqing's characteristics. Since the outlook of Hongya Cave resemblance to the Onsen House in Hayao Miyazaki's animation "Spirited Away", it has exploded in popularity among tourists.

Transportation：
Take a 40-minute taxi ride from High Speed Rail Chongqingxi Station.

Huangjueping Graffiti Street

Address：
Huangjueping Main Street, Jiulongpo District, Chongqing

Content：
The Graffiti Street starts from the Huangjueping Railway Hospital and ends at the 501 Art Gallery (near Sichuan Fine Arts Institute). Stretching 1.25 kilometres, it is one of the best graffiti clusters in China. The entire project was completed by over 800 workers, students and artists. After 150 days of planning and creation, a total of 37 buildings were replaced with current new outlooks.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Chongqingxi Station, walk for about 15 minutes to Chongqingxi Bus Station. Take Bus 441 towards Huangjueping. Get off at Huangjueping Main Street Station and walk for about 1 minute.

Alternatively, you may take a 39-minute taxi ride from Chongqingxi Station.

Testbed 2 Arts Center

Address：
1 E'ling Main Street, Yuzhong District, Chongqing

Content：
Formerly the "Central Bank Printing Factory" during the period of the Republic of China, it was dedicated to printing essential items such as stamps, tax stamps and banknotes, making it an important memory for the old generation in Chongqing. In early 2014, the area was converted into Testbed 2 Arts Center and was appeared in the movie "I Belonged to You". Since then, it has turned into a place for hipsters.

Transportation：
Take a 32-minute taxi ride from High Speed Rail Chongqingxi Station.

The Dazu Rock Carvings

Address：
Baoding Town, Dazu District, Chongqing

Content：
Dazu Rock Carvings is one of the UNESCO World Heritage site in China, which constitutes part of the Chinese cave art history together with other stone carving sites such as Dunhuang, Yungang and Longmen. The stone carvings in the Dazu area range from secular to religious, vividly reflecting the daily social life in ancient China, while proving the harmonious coexistence of Buddhism, Taoism and Confucianism in the past times.

Transportation：
Take a taxi ride for about 1 hour and 22 minutes from High Speed Rail Chongqingxi Station.

Jiefangbei Square

Address：
177 Minzu Road, Yuzhong District, Chongqing

Content：
Used to be a historical site to the victory of the Second Sino-Japanese War and Chongqing's liberation, Jiefangbei Square has now transformed to be the most prosperous business district in Chongqing. The major shopping malls nearby include Chongqing Department Store and Chongqing Times Square with all kinds of goods available. In addition, if you don't want to miss out on the local delicacies in Chongqing, Bayi Road (food street) is surely the place to be.

Transportation：
Take a 42-minute taxi ride from High Speed Rail Chongqingxi Station.
