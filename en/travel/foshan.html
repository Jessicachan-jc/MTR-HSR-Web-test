Nanfeng Ancient Kiln

Address：
6 Gaomiao Road, Shiwan Town, Foshan

Content：
The main attractions of the Nanfeng Ancient Kiln include Nanfeng Ancient Stove, Guangdong Shiwan Ceramic Museum, High Stove, God Banya, The Lin's Hall and buildings from the Ming and Qing Dynasties. The scenic area covers over 1,000 acres, where visitors may enjoy the professional performance of the pottery master or experience the fun of making pottery in the pottery centre.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Foshanxi Station, walk about 200 metres to Foshanxi Bus Station and take Bus K3 towards Lingnan Avenue. Get off at Haikou Bus Station and walk for about 16 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 39-minute taxi ride from Foshanxi Station.

Foshan Grand Theatre

Address：
1 Huakang Road, Lecong Town, Shunde District, Foshan

Content：
The Grand Theatre is adjacent to the Century Lotus Sports Centre and the Youth Culture Palace. Equipped with the highest level of architectural acoustics and stage installation in the country, the audience is assured of enjoying a top-notch concert in the theatre.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Foshanxi Station, walk about 200 metres to Foshanxi Bus Station and take Bus K340 towards Lecong Traffic Centre. Get off at the terminal and change to Bus 343 towards Poly East Bay North Station. Get off at Century Lotus Sports Centre (West Gate) Bus Station and walk for 10 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 50-minute taxi ride from Foshanxi Station.

Foshan Zumiao Museum (The Ancestral Temple)

Address：
21 Zumiao Road, Chancheng District, Foshan

Content：
Foshan people have a saying, "If you don't go to Zumiao in Foshan, you have not been to Foshan." The museum includes the ancient complex of Ancestral Temple, Confucius Temple, Ip Man Hall and Huang Feihong Memorial Hall. It is a folk museum which combines architectural art and traditional culture.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Foshanxi Station, walk about 200 metres to Foshanxi Bus Station and take Bus G12 towards Lingnan Avenue Bus Terminal. Get off at Zumiao Station "A" and walk for about 2 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 38-minute taxi ride from Foshanxi Station.

Foshan Creative Industry Park (Jihua 4th Road)

Address：
33 Jihua 4th Road, Chancheng District, Foshan

Content：
The creative park, which was converted from 12 old factories, covers an area of over 100 acres and has a construction area of 250,000 square metres. At present, over 100 companies are stationed in the park, ranging from ceramics research and design, finance, catering to leisure and entertainment business.

Transportation：
From High Speed Rail Foshanxi Station, walk about 200 metres to Foshanxi Bus Station and take Bus K3 towards Lingnan Avenue. Get off at Jihua 4th Road East Station and walk for about 10 minutes.

Alternatively, you may take a 33-minute taxi ride from Foshanxi Station.
