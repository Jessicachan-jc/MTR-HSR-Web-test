Introduction

Located in the downtown area, Hong Kong West Kowloon Station provides easy access to transportation with pick up and drop off facilities for franchised bus, minibus, taxi, private car and non-franchised bus.

The Station is also connected to the MTR West Rail Line Austin Station and Tung Chung Line / Airport Express Kowloon Station via the overhead bridge or pedestrian tunnel. The Hong Kong West Kowloon Station Bus Terminus and Kowloon Station Public Transport Interchange located nearby provide bus and minibus services to / from different districts. Details are available on Transport Department's website.

MTR

The MTR network covers all major districts of Hong Kong. The Hong Kong West Kowloon Station provides direct access to the MTR Austin Station and Kowloon Station via the overhead bridge or pedestrian tunnel, facilitating a seamless transfer on the MTR.

For more information on MTR services, visit the MTR website www.mtr.com.hk

Franchised Bus

There are over 40 franchised bus routes serving the West Kowloon Station Bus Terminus, the Kowloon Station Public Transport Interchange, Canton Road and Jordan Road.

Green Minibuses

Currently, there are a few green minibus routes that stop over the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station and surrounding areas.

Non-franchised Bus

Non-franchised buses may make use of the lay-by at Wui Man Road for passenger drop-off / pick-up. Parking spaces are available near West Kowloon Station Bus Terminus.

Map

Taxi

Taxi passengers going to West Kowloon Station can get off at the Station’s B1 level. Passengers who wish to take a taxi should go to B2 level where the taxi stand with queueing facility is located.
