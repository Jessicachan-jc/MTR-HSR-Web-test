Online ticketing service provided by MTR and 12306 China Railway

    In addition to MTR's online ticketing, you can also enjoy online ticketing convenience through the 12306 website and mobile app provided by China Railway.

    Notes to ticket collection at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station:

    • 12306 Ticket Collection Machines are available at the station. The machine accepts PRC Resident Identity Card, 2nd generation Mainland Travel Permit for Hong Kong and Macao Residents (Home Return Permit) and 2nd generation Mainland Travel Permit for Taiwan Residents.
    • Travellers with 1st generation travel documents or other travel documents, please collect tickets from ticketing counters operated by China Railway (Hong Kong) with a handling fee ranging from HK$10 to HK$30 subject to the ticket fares of each ticket.
