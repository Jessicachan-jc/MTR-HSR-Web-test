Student Tickets

Student Tickets are available for Hong Kong students studying in higher education institutions in the Mainland with no wages or income and who are holders of Train Ticket Student Discount Card attached to their valid student cards. Qualified students can buy Student Tickets for 4 single journeys each year for second class seats on trains travelling between their home and the place of their college (or the place where they are undertaking an internship) during summer break (from 1 June to 30 September) and winter break (from 1 December to 31 March) each year. Newly enrolled students can use their confirmation of enrolment while fresh graduates can use their college’s written proof to buy Student Tickets once. The Implemented Fare for Student Ticket will be approximately 25% discount of the Published Fare of Adult Ticket.

Student Tickets can be purchased at the ticketing counter operated by China Railway (HK) Holdings Ltd. at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station. For more information, please contact China Railway (HK) Holdings Ltd.

Disabled Military Tickets

Disabled Military Tickets (for all classes of travel) are available to holders of valid Certificate for Disabled Military of the People's Republic of China or Certificate of the People's Republic of China for Disabled Policemen. The Implemented Fare for Disabled Military Ticket will be approximately half of the Published Fare of Adult Fare.

Disabled Military Tickets can be purchased at the ticketing counter operated by China Railway (HK) Holdings Ltd. at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station. For more information, please contact China Railway (HK) Holdings Ltd.
