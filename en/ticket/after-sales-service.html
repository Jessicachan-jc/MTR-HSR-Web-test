    Booking Enquiry

    Travellers can check the booking status of their ticket purchase via the original ticketing channel, at ticketing counters at the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station or by calling the hotline on 2120 0888.

    Alteration

    For the convenience of travellers, tickets can be altered once before train departure. No service charge is required.

    • Only departure date, train number and class of travel can be altered
    • If a booking contains multiple tickets, alteration can be made for selected tickets
    • If the fare after alteration is higher than the original one, travellers will be required to top up the difference; if the fare after alteration is lower than the original one, the difference will be refunded
    • For alteration at stations or ticket agents, travellers must produce the original or a photocopy of the personal identity document used for the booking for verification
    • Tickets cannot be refunded after alteration

    Alteration of Tickets Purchased via Hong Kong Ticketing Channels

    Alteration of tickets via Hong Kong ticketing channels will be based on the original currency of the booking. For alteration of tickets which are sold based on the HKD fare, the fare for the new ticket will be the valid HKD Implemented Fare for the current month. Therefore, the new ticket fare may not be the same as the original one even if it is only another train of the same class of travel on the same day. For tickets purchased using a credit card, UnionPay or mobile payment tied to a credit card, alteration must be made using the original credit card.

    For tickets bought via Hong Kong ticketing channel that have already been collected, alteration can be made at Mainland train stations. The relevant service will be provided based on fare in RMB. Please take note of the following:

    • Ticket alteration should be processed no later than 30 minutes before train departure
    • For tickets with Hong Kong West Kowloon as departure station, the alteration cut-off time is 60 minutes before train departure

    Alteration of Tickets Purchased via Mainland Ticketing Channels

    Uncollected tickets can be altered through the 12306 website or mobile app, or at designated ticketing counters of Mainland stations. Please take note of the following:

    • Ticket alteration should be processed no later than 30 minutes before train departure times
    • For tickets with Hong Kong West Kowloon as departure station, the alteration cut-off time is 60 minutes before train departure.

    Tickets that have already been collected can be altered at the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station. The relevant services will be provided based on the fare in RMB. Please take note of the following:

    • Ticket alteration should be processed no later than 60 minutes before train departure
    • Tickets must be purchased in cash. Difference in fare will be topped up or refunded in in the form of cash in RMB.
    • For tickets purchased using any payment method other than cash, the fare after alteration must be the same as the original payment

    Refund

    You can refund any unutilised and unaltered tickets. Travellers will receive a proportion of the fare as a refund depending on the time before departure. Refunds are processed in dollars and any amount less than RMB1 or HKD1 will be rounded up to the nearest integer. For refunds, travellers must produce the original or a photocopy of the personal identity document used for the booking for verification.

    Before train departure Refund ratio
    Within 48 hours to cut off time of respective channels for refund 50% of paid fare
    48 hours to 14 days 70% of paid fare
    15 days or above 95% of paid fare

    Refund of Tickets Purchased in Hong Kong

    Refund of tickets purchased via Hong Kong ticketing channels will be processed in the original currency and based on the fare on the date of purchase. Refunds cannot be processed via ticket machines, online ticketing and tele-ticketing. Please collect your tickets at the ticketing counters at the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station before processing a refund.

    Refund of Tickets Purchased via Mainland Ticketing Channels

    Uncollected tickets can be refunded through the 12306 website or mobile app, or at designated ticketing counters of Mainland stations. Ticket alteration should be processed no later than 30 minutes before train departure times. For tickets with Hong Kong West Kowloon as departure station, the alteration cut-off time is 60 minutes before train departure.

    Tickets purchased in cash that have already been collected can be refunded at the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station. The relevant services will be provided based on the fare in RMB and refunds will be made in the form of cash in RMB.
