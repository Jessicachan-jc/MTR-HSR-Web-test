High Speed Rail special fare HK$40 to Shenzhen for the elderly

    The High Speed Rail Elderly Promotion is back with a greater choice of trains to Shenzhen. The elderly can enjoy a High Speed Rail special fare of HK$40* per single trip for travelling between Hong Kong West Kowloon Station and Futian Station on designated trains. To enjoy the offer, simply present Hong Kong Senior Citizen Card and valid travel document upon ticket purchase at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station. Only 14 minutes from Hong Kong with High Speed Rail, set off to Shenzhen now for travel hot spots and popular food!

    Travel Hot Spots Recommendation:

    Xianhu Botanical Garden

    The garden is divided into six scenic areas with different species of plants. The most popular one is the cactus in the Desert Plant Area.

    Transport: From High Speed Rail Futian Station, take Metro Line 2 and get off at Huangbeiling Station. Then take a 20-minute taxi ride.

    MixC World

    The mall is a must-see spot in Shenzhen for its art, fashion, culture, food and shopping. The elephant art installation at the main entrance is a check-in hot spot.

    Transport: From High Speed Rail Futian Station, take Metro Line 11. Change to Line 1 at Chegongmiao Station and get off at Hi-Tech Park Station. From Exit A, walk for about 8 minutes.

    OCT Harbour ‧ OCT Creative Exhibition Center

    OCT Harbour is a comprehensive leisure and shopping area with a theme of marine culture and ecological environment. OCT Creative Exhibition Center is one of the landmark buildings.

    Transport: From High Speed Rail Futian Station, take Metro Line 11. Change to Line 9 at Hongshuwan South Station and get off at Shenzhen Bay Park Station. From Exit D1, walk for about 11 minutes.
