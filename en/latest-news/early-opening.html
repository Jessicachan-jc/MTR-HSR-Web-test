Station Early Opening on 23 September

    To facilitate traveller's journey on the Commencement Date (23 September), Hong Kong West Kowloon Station will open early with arrangement as follows:

    1. Entrance D will open at 5:00am;
    2. All other entrances and station carpark will open at 5:10am

    Ticketing Counters and Ticket Machines will start service at 5:10am that day.
