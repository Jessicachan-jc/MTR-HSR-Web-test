High Speed Rail – Official Rail of China Import and Export Fair

    As the Official Rail of 125th-126th China Import and Export Fair (Canton Fair), High Speed Rail ensures you a speedy journey to Guangzhou from Hong Kong West Kowloon Station as fast as 47 minutes. Exhibitors and participants from Hong Kong and overseas can enjoy High Speed Rail's frequent train service with extra convenience.

    Getting to Canton Fair from Hong Kong with High Speed Rail:

    1. To Hong Kong West Kowloon Station
      From downtown Hong Kong: Take MTR to West Rail Line Austin Station or Tung Chung Line Kowloon Station, walk across the footbridges or through the subways to arrive at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station.
      From Hong Kong International Airport: Take Airport Express and get off at Kowloon Station in approximately 22 minutes. Take Free Airport Express Shuttle Bus K1 or walk across the footbridges to arrive at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station.
    2. Take High Speed Rail to Guangzhounan Station
      34 round-trip trains are available to travel daily between Hong Kong West Kowloon Station and Guangzhounan Station. Travellers may take the Vibrant Express trains operated by MTR Corporation, with spacious design and thoughtful on-board facilities and amenities such as Free Wi-Fi and power sockets, to enhance the travel experience.
    3. To China Import and Export Fair Complex
      Take taxi from Guangzhounan Station or take Metro Line 2 to Changgang Station, then transfer to Line 8 and exit at Xingang Dong Station (to Area A) or Pazhou Station (to Area B or C).

    Friendly reminder: Please reserve sufficient time for ticket purchase or collection as well as immigration clearance procedures at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station during the fair period. For tickets purchased via MTR Website, hotline and ticketing agent, no matter outbound or return tickets, please collect them at Hong Kong West Kowloon Station at least 45 minutes before train departs.
