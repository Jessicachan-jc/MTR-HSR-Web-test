Parking Charges & Park-and-Ride Discount Offer

    Station car park is located at B2 level of the station. Discount scheme is available for High Speed Rail "Park and Ride" users.

    Station Car Park Map

    Parking Rate:

    Normal: $32 / hour

    High Speed Rail Park & Ride: $100 / day

    MTR Park & Ride: $17 / hour

    Day Parking: $130 (from 08:00 to 20:00)

    Night Parking: $100 (from 20:00 to 08:00)

    Payment Method: Octopus, VISA or MasterCard (Octopus only for MTR Park & Ride)



    Terms & Conditions:

    1. For each car park transaction, the maximum transaction amount for VISA is HK$500 (HK$190 for credit cards issued by overseas card issuers) while the maximum transaction amount for MasterCard is HK$500 (HK$300 for credit cards issued by overseas card issuers).
    2. For all hourly charged parking, a partial hour will be charged as a full hour.
    3. This car park offers up to 15 minutes free parking for pick-up / drop-off purposes; parking fee will be charged for parking exceeding 15 minutes (including the first 15 minutes).
    4. High Speed Rail Park & Ride rate is charged on daily basis (consecutive 24-hour basis from parking time) with a maximum parking duration of 14 days; a partial day will be charged as a full day. Normal hourly rates will apply thereafter.
    5. For High Speed Rail Park & Ride, please redeem the offer by presenting a valid High Speed Rail train ticket (to / from Hong Kong West Kowloon Station) issued during the parking period at the car park shroff office (near car park exit) before payment is made.
    6. For MTR Park & Ride offer, the same Octopus must be used for car park entries / exits and MTR ride (except Light Rail, MTR Bus and MTR Feeder Bus) during the parking period. The Octopus should not have more than nine transaction records between the MTR ride and the payment of parking fee.
    7. The MTR Park & Ride offer is available for 1 day only, starting from 05:30 on the parking day to 01:00 the following day. A normal hourly rate will apply for non-specified hours.
    8. Day Parking fee is applicable from Mondays to Sundays from 08:00 to 20:00 on parking days.
    9. Night Parking fee is applicable from Mondays to Sundays from 20:00 on parking days to 08:00 the following day.
    10. MTR Park & Ride and High Speed Rail Park & Ride offers cannot be used in conjunction with any other offers unless otherwise specified.
    11. MTR Corporation Limited or the car park operator reserves the right to alter or terminate these offers at any time and reserves the right to make the final decision in the event of any dispute.
    12. The Contracts (Rights of Third Parties) Ordinance shall not apply to the Terms and Conditions herein.
