Art in Station

ART IN MTR - HONG KONG WEST KOWLOON STATION

There are high quality art pieces to enrich the travelling environment installed through the Hong Kong West Kowloon Station (WEK).

6 artworks by established local and international artists from Mainland China, South Korea, France, Morocco and Hong Kong serve as memorable focal points in various locations inside WEK.
