Important Information

1. Before your travel, please ensure you are aware of the latest travel restrictions and preventative measures or health quarantine arrangements in place at your destination and plan your journey accordingly.

2. For the health and safety of everyone, you are required to wear a mask inside Hong Kong West Kowloon Station and when travelling on the High Speed Rail.Always keep social distancing when in crowded places.

3. When you enter Hong Kong West Kowloon Station your temperature will be checked using Infrared body temperature screening equipment. If you show signs of a fever (body temperature over 37.3°C), you will be refused entry to the station.

4. Maintain good personal hygiene at all times. If you travel outside Hong Kong and have a fever or other symptoms upon your return, consult a doctor as soon as possible and inform the doctor of your travel history.。

To prevent the spread of Coronavirus, we have enhanced infection preventative measures in the station and on trains. Click here to learn more.
