Frequenty Asked Questions

How many tickets can I buy online each time?

The maximum number of tickets that a customer can buy on this website is ten. A Round Trip comprises two tickets, so only a maximum of five Round Trips can be booked each time.

Can I buy a Non-cross Boundary Ticket online?

No. This website is only for the booking of tickets departing from or arriving at West Kowloon Station in Hong Kong. Travellers looking to buy a Non-cross Boundary Ticket can visit the special counters (China Railway Corporation) located at West Kowloon Station or www.12306.cn for online ticketing. Please note that tickets bought from the 12306 website must be collected at a train station in Mainland China.

Can I buy just one Child Ticket?

No. Child Tickets must be booked together with an Adult Ticket. A Child Ticket can be purchased using a valid travel document of the child or accompanying adult.

When can I book an online ticket?

Under normal circumstances, online ticketing is available from 30 days before the date of departure to 90 minutes before the time of departure.

Can I buy a ticket online using a digital wallet?

No. An online ticket purchase can only be made using a credit card, including Visa, MasterCard and UnionPay. Virtual credit cards are not accepted.

Is there any administrative fee for online ticketing?

There is no administrative fee to be paid for online ticketing.

Can I select seats, such as a special seat for a wheelchair passenger, while buying a ticket online?

No. For online ticketing, seats will be automatically allocated by the ticketing system. Travellers who require special assistance, such as a special seat for a wheelchair passenger, can visit the ticketing office at West Kowloon Station or call the customer service hotline: 21200888.

Can I buy multiple tickets for the same journey?

No. As the real name of the traveller is required for each ticket, travellers can only buy one ticket in that name for the same journey on the same day.

When buying a Round Trip ticket, can the name of the traveller for the outbound and return journey be different?

No. The date, train number and class of travel for a single booking must be the same. For a Round Trip ticket, the name of the traveller for the outbound and return journey must also be the same.

Is online ticketing available 24 hours a day?

No. The system will undergo maintenance from 11:00 pm to 6:00 am every day. During this time, only train number information would be available.

Can I make changes to my ticket online?

You can only make online changes to tickets bought on this website. You can only make changes to the date, train number and class of travel of a ticket and that must be done at least 90 minutes before the time of departure. If you have made a ticket purchase online, but have yet to collect your ticket, you can make changes to the ticket at least 90 minutes before the time of departure via the ticketing and customer service hotline (21200888) by entering your order number and ticket collection password. If you did not set a password for your booking, you can process the changes by bringing your ticket and original/copy of your identification document to the customer service counter at West Kowloon Station or Admiralty Station or other designated service channels.

After making changes, the ticket cannot be refunded. If necessary, refer to the regulations of the “Trans-Hong Kong High Speed Rail Traveller Transportation Organisation Rules”

Can I refund a ticket online?

No. Refunds cannot be processed online. If necessary, refer to the regulations of the “Trans-Hong Kong High Speed Rail Traveller Transportation Organisation Rules”

Where do I collect my ticket after buying it online?

Tickets bought on this website must be collected at designated locations in Hong Kong. Travellers must collect their tickets at least xxxx minutes before time of departure by showing the original travel document used for their booking at the ticketing office at West Kowloon Station or customer service counter at Admiralty Station. For tickets on a journey arriving at West Kowloon Station, please ensure sufficient time allowance so that you can catch the train after collecting your ticket. Those travelling with a Mainland Travel Permit for Hong Kong and Macao Residents or Resident Identity Card of the People’s Republic of China can collect their tickets at a ticketing machine at West Kowloon Station at least xxxx minutes before time of departure with the credit card number used for the booking or order number and ticket collection password.

Why does the system show that the session has expired and the ticketing process cannot be completed?

The entire ticketing process must be completed within 30 minutes from train number selection.

Can I make changes to just some of the tickets in an order?

Yes.

Can I collect just some of the tickets in an order first?

Yes. Tickets in a single order can be collected in batches.

For an order comprising an Adult Ticket and a Child Ticket, can I make changes to just the linked Adult Ticket?

Yes. If a Child Ticket is linked to an adult identification document and there are changes to the adult identification document, the Child Ticket must be collected with the original adult identification document. In addition, an order that contains a Child Ticket must include at least one Adult Ticket.
