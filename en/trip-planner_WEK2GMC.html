Fares are set in Renminbi with the Hong Kong Dollar (HKD) fare being adjusted monthly subject to the prevailing exchange rate.

Adjusted HKD fares will be announced on the first day of every month. For tickets sold in HKDs, the fare is the valid Implemented Fare for the current month which may not be the same as the fare on the date of travel.

The Implemented Fare for First, Premium and Business Class tickets for travel between Hong Kong West Kowloon and Guangzhounan or Humen on the Long-haul trains will be higher than fares of the Short-haul trains.

All fares are subject to confirmation by China Railway Corporation.
